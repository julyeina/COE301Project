Hello player! Welcome to: So You Think You’re a Longhorn! 
You are tasked with finding different landmarks on the map (represented by symbols and special characters). 
You will only have five minutes to complete your quest…. Unless you find a power up! 
Power ups are scattered across the map that grant you extra time or give you double or nothing points. 
By the end you’ll find out how much of a longhorn you are (we're going to weed you out)!

How to play:
To walk you will type the direction and number of spaces you’d like to travel (ex: w10)
To interact with a landmark you will walk directly on top of it’s space

(P.S. there may or may not be a hidden passage somewhere on the map…)
